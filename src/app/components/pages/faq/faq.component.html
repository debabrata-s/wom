What is Upkeep?

Upkeep is your home preventive maintenance partner. We provide an assessment and record of all the critical components of your home, as well as information when you are considering major upgrades or repair. With our Services that can be added to your Preventive Maintenance visit, we can even handle some of the minor repairs and upgrades you never quite get around to. Our goal is to help you extend the life of your home's key elements, help you avoid unexpected repairs and free up your time for the more important parts of your life.

Why choose Upkeep?

We are the pros. We hire only the best people and make sure they are skilled and fully vetted. Unlike some services that outsource work to independent contractors, our Upkeep Pros are our employees, who have gone through rigorous background checks and have proven they have the necessary skills to complete the work.

What is the Home Review?

Our proprietary State of Your Home Review is a description of the condition of each of the key elements of your home. For each, there is an evaluation of the condition and any action we would recommend. Everything is clearly labeled with the familiar green, yellow, red “stoplight chart,” making it easy to scan the status. The review is yours to keep at the end of our visit and is the process we use for each home visit.

We charge based on the size of your home and have two Preventive. Prices range from $599 to $1899 per year and are billed quarterly. After our Upkeep Pro completes the Home Review, they will let you know the exact price based on your square footage. Learn more about pricing here.

How do you charge for a Preventive Maintenance Contract?

We bill quarterly for the Preventive Maintenance Contract. We charge for the initial visit when you sign up and at the time of each subsequent quarterly visit. You have the option for automatic quarterly billing or to pay at the end of each visit. Want to keep your card on file for easy payment? Just let your Upkeep Pro know at the end of your visit. Your Preventive Maintenance Contract can be canceled at any time.
